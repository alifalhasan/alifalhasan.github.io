**Alif Al Hasan** is a **Ph.D. student in Computer Science** at **Case Western Reserve University**, working under the supervision of **[Prof. Sumon Biswas](https://sumonbis.github.io/)** at the **[reSAID Lab (Responsible Software and AI Design Lab)](https://resaid-lab.github.io/)**. His current research specializes in **Software Engineering and Large Language Models (LLMs)**, with a specific focus on **autonomous agent safety**.

He earned his **Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Computer Science and Engineering** from **Jahangirnagar University**, where he was awarded the **NST Fellowship** for his Master’s research. Building on a strong foundation in **algorithm design and large-scale data analysis**, Alif is also deeply involved in **competitive programming**, actively continuing to mentor students for national-level contests.

Alif's work has been published in **top-tier conferences and journals**, including **ASE 2026**, **ICSE SEET 2026**, and **EASE 2026**. Passionate about **research, innovation, and problem-solving**, Alif consistently contributes to both **academic advancements and the programming community**.
